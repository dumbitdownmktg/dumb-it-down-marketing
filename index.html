Why does this even exist?

Most marketing content is written for people who already understand marketing. Which is great if you have a marketing degree. Most small business owners don't.

You're running a martial arts school, a plumbing company, a gym. You're great at what you do. But somewhere between running the business and living your life, someone told you that you need to be on Facebook, Google, Instagram, TikTok, and also have a podcast.

This site exists to cut through that noise. No funnels that take 45 minutes to explain. No buzzwords. Just real tools you can actually use to understand where your customers are coming from and what it's costing you to get them.

Take what's useful. Leave what isn't. And if this saves you even one bad marketing spend, it did its job.
